The department also offers Ph. D programme, for which candidates are selected through NET/GATE or ISM Entrance Test, also offers a number of courses in various B. Tech and M. Tech programmes.
It is actively involved in R & D programmes in the frontier areas of Applied Research on:

  1. Fly ash characterization and utilization
  2. Clay catalysis
  3. Ceramics Materials
  4. Surface coatings/surface modification
  5. Prevention of scale formation in oil wells
  6. Corrosion science and technology
  7. Tribology
  8. Synthetic Inorganic / Organometallic Chemistry and Catalysis
  9. Arsenic Contamination
  10. Environmental Pollution
  11. Heterogeneous Catalysis and Nano materials synthesis
  12. Supramolecular Chemistry
  13. Supramolecular/Host-guest chemistry
  14. Molecular Recognition
  15. Heterocyclic Chemistry, Organic Synthesis and Organic Methodology
  16. Polymer Chemistry, Biomaterials, Nano composites
  17. Organic Synthesis, Organic Photochemistry
Students obtaining M. Phil / Ph. D from the Department are almost 100% absorbed in industry or academic institution.
